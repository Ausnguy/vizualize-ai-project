Founder's Narrative

Why I Built This

As a student entering the workforce, I noticed a huge problem. Companies collect massive amounts of data, but only a few people actually know how to access it.

I watched smart managers wait 3–5 days just to get a simple sales report because they had to request help from the technology department. I realized that data shouldn't be locked behind a skill barrier. If you can send a text, you should be able to get answers from your database. That’s why I built VizuAlize.ai.

Rubric: Innovative AI Solution

It’s not magic. It’s translation.

VizuAlize.ai connects you directly to your company's data storage (like Snowflake or AWS). You type in plain English, and our software translates it into the code the computer understands.
